01 / EXPLORE

TREKKING

Mountains have always been my reset.

I've explored a range of trails across Karnataka, including Kumara Parvatha (Kukke–Kukke route), Savandurga, Kaivara Betta, and the Gokarna Beach Trek.

Growing up in Garhwal, Uttarakhand, I was naturally drawn to the mountains early on. Local treks during childhood built a lasting connection with high-altitude landscapes.

Today, trekking remains a constant; a space to disconnect, reflect, and push limits.

✈️

02 / EXPERIENCE

TRAVEL

I enjoy exploring diverse landscapes; from coastal towns to mountain regions.

Some of the places I've travelled include Gokarna (solo), Alleppey, Chikkamagaluru, Coorg, Chennai, Kochi, and multiple cities around Bangalore and Mysore.

Beyond the south, I've also travelled across North India; including Delhi, Agra, Rajasthan, and Lucknow; along with a trip to Nepal.

Each place offers a different perspective, shaping how I observe culture, environment, and people.

⚽

03 / MOVE

SPORTS

Sports have always played a key role in keeping me active and competitive.

I regularly play outdoor sports like football, cricket, and pickleball. Indoors, I enjoy games like foosball and other casual formats.

Beyond fitness, sports have helped me build discipline, teamwork, and a balanced mindset.

🏍️

04 / FREEDOM

BIKES

Biking represents freedom and exploration in its simplest form.

I've been on multiple rides, including a memorable trip to Coorg, and plan to explore more routes over time.

I'm currently planning to get my own bike; this section will continue to evolve with future rides and experiences.
